My Background

I am a Communication graduate from UC Santa Barbara, where I earned my Bachelor of Arts degree with a minor in Professional Multimedia Writing in June 2024, graduating with a 3.89 GPA and Dean's Honors recognition. My coursework in areas such as Mass Media, Journalism, Public Speaking, Argumentation, and Global Writing built a strong foundation in communication and critical thinking — skills I carry into my work in higher education administration every day

During my time at UCSB, I was actively involved in campus life through clubs such as the Mindfulness Club, Excursion Club, and the HR Club, which gave me an early appreciation for how community, connection, and student engagement shape the university experience. That perspective now informs how I approach my work supporting students through some of the most important milestones of their academic journeys.

Outside of work, I stay grounded through time in nature, photography, painting, and writing — pursuits that keep me creative, curious, and intentional in both my personal and professional life. My path from student to higher education professional has deepened my commitment to serving others, maintaining excellence, and contributing meaningfully to the academic communities I am part of.

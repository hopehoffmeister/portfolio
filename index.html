Degree Audit and Graduation Advisor

During my time as a Degree Audit & Graduation Advisor at the University of San Francisco, I play a vital role in supporting the operations of the Graduation Center within the Office of the University Registrar and enhancing the student experience. I work across multiple communication channels, including phone, email, chat, and in-person interactions, serving as the first point of contact for students seeking guidance on graduation requirements and processes. Additionally, I conduct comprehensive degree audits for 200–400 students per semester, ensuring accurate and timely graduation eligibility determinations while maintaining organized and up-to-date academic records in compliance with university policies. One of my key contributions is advising students on their academic progress through clear written and verbal communication, while efficiently managing a high volume of requests and processing forms such as program changes and academic exceptions. Furthermore, I coordinate commencement ceremonies for over 3,500 students annually, overseeing event logistics and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless execution. Overall, my work contributes to improved service delivery, student success, and operational efficiency within the registrar’s office.

Academic Services Assistant

During my time as an Academic Services Assistant at the University of San Francisco, I played a vital role in supporting the daily operations of the Office of the Registrar and enhancing the student experience. I worked across multiple communication channels, including phone, email, chat, and in-person interactions, to provide accurate information and assist students, faculty, and staff. Additionally, I contributed to maintaining organized and up-to-date student records, ensuring accuracy and compliance with university policies. One of my key contributions was supporting processes related to registration, enrollment, and academic record updates while managing a high volume of requests efficiently. Furthermore, I assisted with classroom scheduling, commencement coordination, and front desk operations, helping ensure smooth and effective office functions. Overall, my work contributed to improved service delivery and operational efficiency within the registrar’s office.

Office Assistant

During my role as an office assistant at the media center within UCSB's Associated Students department, I demonstrated strong communication skills by efficiently handling incoming phone calls and directing them appropriately. Operating the front desk, I managed incoming traffic and provided guidance to students as needed, ensuring smooth and organized operations within the media center. My responsibilities encompassed facilitating the flow of information and assisting visitors with their inquiries, contributing to a welcoming and helpful environment for all students interacting with the office.

Social Media and Marketing Internships

During my marketing and communications experience with Olami and Anderson Aquatics, I played a vital role in supporting brand growth and audience engagement through a variety of digital and print initiatives. I worked collaboratively with a diverse cohort of interns and team members to brainstorm, develop, and promote mixed-media content across platforms such as Instagram and TikTok. One of my key contributions was assisting in the development and implementation of targeted marketing strategies tailored to specific audiences. Furthermore, I created print materials for distribution to healthcare professionals and the general public, strengthening outreach and communication efforts. Overall, my work contributed to increased engagement, brand awareness, and the success of marketing initiatives across both organizations.
